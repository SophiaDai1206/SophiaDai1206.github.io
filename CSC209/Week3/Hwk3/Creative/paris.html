Welcome to Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light" and is one of the most beautiful and romantic cities in the world. Famous for its art, fashion, and architecture, Paris is home to iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.
